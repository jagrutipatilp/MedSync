Protecting Your Privacy

Website

What Can You Share?

  • Share accurate health information from trusted sources.
  • Avoid posting misinformation, unverified health tips, or medical advice without credentials.
  • Use appropriate language and avoid graphic or violent content.
Website

Using Our AI Responsibly

  • Ask clear, relevant questions for accurate responses.
  • Avoid sharing sensitive medical data with AI chat for privacy reasons.
  • Understand that AI provides general advice and is not a substitute for professional medical consultation.
Website

How to Report Concerns

  • If you see inappropriate behavior, report it using the "Report" button on posts or messages.
  • For technical issues, contact our support team at contact page.
  • Reports will be reviewed promptly to maintain a safe environment.

Please note that the content shared on this platform, including AI responses, is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for personalized guidance.

By using MedSync, you agree to follow these guidelines. Let’s work together to create a supportive, health-focused community!
